# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: March 2026

## Overview

TaskFlow ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the App.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

TaskFlow is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do **not**:

- Collect personal information such as your name, email, or phone number
- Track your location or device information
- Collect analytics or usage data
- Use advertising networks or third-party tracking services
- Share any data with external services or third parties
- Require account creation or user authentication
- Access your contacts, photos, or other device data (except as explicitly needed for features you enable)

## Information Stored Locally

All data created within the App is stored exclusively on your device using AsyncStorage. This includes:

- Task titles, descriptions, and notes
- Due dates and times
- Priority levels and categories
- Completion status
- User preferences and settings

**This data never leaves your device** and is not transmitted to any server or external service.

## Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

| Permission | Purpose | Optional |
|-----------|---------|----------|
| **Notifications** | To deliver task reminders at scheduled times | Yes |
| **Calendar** | To integrate with your device calendar (future feature) | Yes |

You can grant or deny these permissions at any time, and can modify them in your device's settings.

## Data Security

Your data is protected through:

- **Local Storage**: Data is stored on your device using the secure AsyncStorage mechanism
- **No Transmission**: Data is never sent over the internet
- **Device Security**: Data protection depends on your device's security settings

## Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

- **In-App Deletion**: Delete individual tasks or all data through the App's interface
- **App Uninstall**: Uninstalling the App removes all associated data from your device
- **Device Reset**: Performing a factory reset on your device removes all App data

## Third-Party Services

TaskFlow does not use third-party services for data collection, analytics, or advertising. The App is self-contained and does not communicate with external services.

## Children's Privacy

TaskFlow does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information to the App, please contact us immediately.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the App and on our website. Your continued use of the App following changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through the GitHub repository or email us directly.

## Compliance

TaskFlow complies with applicable privacy laws and regulations including:

- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
- Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
